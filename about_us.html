Airport Overview

Our Story

Founded in 1965, SkyHarbor International Airport has grown from a regional airfield to one of the world's premier aviation hubs. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us a gateway for millions of travelers each year.

Today, we serve over 50 million passengers annually and connect to more than 200 destinations worldwide. Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated team ensure a seamless travel experience for all.

Our Mission

To provide world-class airport services that connect people and businesses safely, efficiently, and sustainably.

Our Vision

To be the world's most admired airport, recognized for innovation, sustainability, and exceptional customer experience.

Our History

1965

Airport Foundation

SkyHarbor International Airport officially opens with two runways and a single terminal.

1982

Terminal Expansion

Second terminal opens, doubling passenger capacity and adding international flights.

1998

Modernization Program

Major renovation begins, introducing advanced security systems and passenger amenities.

2010

Sustainability Initiative

Airport achieves carbon neutrality and implements comprehensive recycling programs.

2020

Terminal 3 Opening

State-of-the-art terminal opens with biometric screening and smart technology.
