Check-in & Security

Check-in Times

  • Domestic flights: 2 hours before departure
  • International flights: 3 hours before departure
  • Online check-in available 24 hours before flight

Security Screening

  • Liquids limited to 100ml containers
  • Sharp objects prohibited in carry-on
  • Laptops must be removed from bags

Customs Regulations

Declare all items over $10,000 USD. Prohibited items include fresh fruits, vegetables, and certain animal products. Check with your destination country for specific restrictions.

Baggage Services

Baggage Allowance

  • Economy: 1 carry-on + 1 personal item
  • Business: 2 carry-on + 1 personal item
  • Checked baggage: 23kg (50lbs) per bag

Storage & Lockers

  • Short-term lockers available in terminals
  • Long-term storage: $10/day per item
  • Prohibited items cannot be stored

Lost Baggage

If your baggage is lost or delayed, immediately report to the Baggage Service Office in the arrivals hall. Keep your baggage tag for reference. Compensation may be available for essential items purchased due to delay.

Special Assistance

Reduced Mobility

  • Wheelchair assistance available
  • Accessible facilities throughout airport
  • Escort services for connecting flights

Family Services

  • Nursing rooms available in all terminals
  • Children's play areas near gates
  • Stroller rental services

Unaccompanied Minors

Children ages 5-11 traveling alone require our Unaccompanied Minor service ($150 fee). Our staff will escort your child through security, to the gate, and ensure they board safely. Please arrive 2 hours before departure to complete paperwork.

Travel Guidelines

Visa Information

  • Check entry requirements for your destination
  • Some countries require visas in advance
  • Allow sufficient time for visa processing

Health & Safety

  • Masks available upon request
  • Sanitizing stations throughout airport
  • Enhanced cleaning procedures in place

COVID-19 Updates

Check destination country requirements for testing, vaccination, or quarantine. Some destinations may require negative COVID-19 test results. Stay updated on changing regulations through official government websites.
