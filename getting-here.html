Getting To & From the Airport

Find the best transport and parking options for your journey.

Transport Options

Short-Term Parking

Perfect for pick-ups, drop-offs, and visits under 24 hours.

  • First 30 minutes Free
  • 1 hour $5
  • 2 hours $10
  • Daily maximum $40
Long-Term Parking

Economical option for extended trips with shuttle service to terminals.

  • Daily rate $15
  • Weekly rate $90
  • Monthly rate $300
  • Free shuttle Every 10 min
Premium Parking

Closest to terminals with valet service and car care options.

  • Daily rate $35
  • Valet service Included
  • Car wash $25
  • EV charging Available
Airport Express Train

Fast and direct connection to downtown in 25 minutes.

  • Frequency Every 15 min
  • Travel time 25 min
  • First train 5:00 AM
  • Last train 12:00 AM
  • Ticket price $18
Airport Shuttle Bus

Connecting the airport with major hotels and city centers.

  • Frequency Every 20 min
  • Travel time 45 min
  • First bus 4:30 AM
  • Last bus 11:30 PM
  • Ticket price $12
Metro Connection

Affordable metro service with connections throughout the city.

  • Frequency Every 10 min
  • Travel time 35 min
  • First train 5:15 AM
  • Last train 1:00 AM
  • Ticket price $3.50
Licensed Taxis

Available 24/7 at designated taxi ranks outside all terminals.

  • Initial charge $3.50
  • Per mile $2.75
  • To downtown ~$45
  • Waiting time $0.50/min
Ride-Sharing

Uber, Lyft, and Bolt services available at designated pickup zones.

  • UberX to downtown ~$35
  • UberXL to downtown ~$55
  • Lyft to downtown ~$32
  • Bolt to downtown ~$30
Pickup Locations

Designated pickup zones for all transport services.

  • Taxis Terminal 1, Arrivals Level
  • Uber/Lyft Terminal 2, Level 1
  • Shuttles All terminals, Ground Transport
  • Private pickups Cell Phone Waiting Lot
Car Rental Companies
